Before you begin

This looks like a phone or small screen. The real CASPer® test is taken on a computer with a keyboard and webcam — please switch to one.

This session mirrors the full CASPer® format:

  • Section 1 — Video responses: 4 scenarios, 2 questions each shown one at a time. Role-play scenarios open with a 10-second screen stating your role. For each question: a 10-second countdown to read it, then 1 minute of recording that starts and stops automatically. No retakes, no playback.
  • Optional 10-minute break, then Section 2 — Typed responses: 7 scenarios, 2 questions shown together, 3:30 to type both, auto-submit at zero. A 5-minute break is offered after the 4th typed scenario.
  • Each scenario is shown for a timed 30-second read, followed by an optional 30-second reflection with the scenario still on screen. You can never go back. Pasting is disabled; typos are not penalized.
  • Recordings upload in the background — a slow connection won't stop the test.

Total: roughly 45–60 minutes without breaks. Quiet room, computer, working webcam and microphone.
